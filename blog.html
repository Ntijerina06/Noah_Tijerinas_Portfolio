Find Happiness Within

Before I start my homework, I wanted to share my new perspective on life. I recently came upon a video of a UFC fighter. I don't know the exact fighter, but he said something that resonated with me. He said something along the lines of " The greatest moment in my life is when I realized I didn't need a belt to be happy." He had just won the UFC World Champion belt, something he had worked his whole life for. It made me really reflect on my source of happiness. Currently, one of the only sources of joy I find is one day getting accepted into a prestigious University, but that shouldn't be the case. I shouldn't let a school dictate my happiness; I should find happiness within and through god. This hasn't shifted my determination to accomplish my goals because I will still give it my all to get accepted into my dream university. Still, besides that, I went to a hackathon. I spent 15-plus hours coding an AI assistant called Nova to help kids learn about space, but sadly, I didn't place. I still learned a lot, but hopefully, I will win the next hackathon I attend.
